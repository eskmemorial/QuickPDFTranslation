Usage

  1. Visit a webpage with a video, for example, this.
  2. Click the video and play it if the video doesn't play automatically.
  3. Press shortcut keys when you are playing a video.

If a video in <iframe> tag plays automatically, you have to click the video to enable shortcut keys.

If the webpage has the same shortcut key as that of this extension, you have to change the shortcut key of this extension.

Default shortcut keys and their functions

  • d : increase playback speed
  • a : decrease playback speed
  • s : set playback speed as default or favorite speed
  • c : advance video by 10 seconds
  • z : rewind video by 10 seconds
  • x : pause video
  • e : increse volume
  • q : decrease volume
  • w : set volume as default
  • r : show controller on the top-left of the video
  • t : reload settings without reloading the webpage
  • v : first press, register "A" time of AB loop. second press, register "B" time of AB loop. third press, clear AB loop.
